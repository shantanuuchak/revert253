biography

Revert, born Brandon Phillips, is a hip hop artist from Lakewood, WA. Reverts music brings witty lyricism with delivery even jimmy johns couldn’t compete with. Growing up in Lakewood, WA, Revert lived about 500 yards from the local skate park where he took up skateboarding at the age of 13. After picking up a skateboard, Revert wanted to do nothing else and had dreams of becoming a professional skateboarder. It wasn’t until he was about 17 years old that he took interest in hip hop. Revert took attention to this and started free styling with his friend for the fun of it. After realizing he was actually very good at free-styling he decided to start writing music.

“My music speaks for itself.”
